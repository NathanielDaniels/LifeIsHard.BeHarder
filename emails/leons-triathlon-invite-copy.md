# Patrick Wingert Subscriber Email Draft

Subject: Race Day Is Coming: Leon's Triathlon, June 7

Preview: Patrick races America's Race, Leon's Triathlon, on Sunday, June 7 in Hammond, Indiana.

## Copy

Dear friends, supporters, sponsors, and community,

On Sunday, June 7, I will be racing at America's Race, Leon's Triathlon in Hammond, Indiana as part of the USA Triathlon Para Development Series.

This race is more than a start line.

It is another opportunity to represent the adaptive athlete community, keep pushing the limits of what is possible in endurance sport, and show what resilience looks like in real time.

Every mile, every transition, and every hard-earned finish is built on the support of people like you. The messages, encouragement, partnerships, and belief from this community matter more than most people realize.

If you are local to the area, I would love for you to come out and cheer alongside the Dare2Tri Elite Team as we take on one of the most meaningful races on the calendar.

Supporters attending race weekend will also get an up-close look at my new Quintana Roo XPR race bike, generously granted to me courtesy of the Challenged Athletes Foundation.

You will also get a behind-the-scenes preview of this year's race kit setup before we hit the start line.

Free high fives and photo ops after the race for everyone who comes out to show support.

## Race Details

Sunday, June 7

Paratriathlon start time: 8:30 AM CDT

Official schedule currently lists the Paratriathlon wave at 8:30 AM and notes that race start waves are subject to change.

Location:

If you need to leave earlier, please park at:

From there, it is a short walk to the venue.

This season is about more than race results.

It is about showing up consistently. Choosing discipline over excuses. Continuing to build visibility for adaptive athletes. Proving that hard things are still worth doing.

If you are able to make it out, your energy on course genuinely makes a difference.

Thank you for being part of this journey.

See you at the race.

Patrick Wingert
Adaptive Para Triathlete
Dare2Tri Elite Team
